Users can sign up for the newsletter via Facebook. Springer uses Facebook Lead Ads to help users easily sign up for this service as it is an easier experience for users. Please be aware that all the personal data is being transferred to facebook to facilitate advertisements. Users can unsubscribe from this service by writing an email to onlineservice@springernature.com 
For Conferences Springer Nature America collects personal data from attendees and uses this mainly to identify the users and notify them on the future conferences. No automated decisions are made using personal data. Users personal data may be used by marketing services to provide users with attractive offers
