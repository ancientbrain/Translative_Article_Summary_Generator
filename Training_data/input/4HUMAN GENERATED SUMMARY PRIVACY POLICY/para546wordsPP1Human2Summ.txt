The privacy policy for scientificamerican.com is summarised as follows:
When a guest user accesses the website then only limited personal data is processed.
When a registered customer logs in then information is collected in order to be able to provide usage reporting to that customer.
When a customer is registered for any services or subscribes to the newsletters, further personal data is processed in the scope of such services.
Personal data may be disclosed to third parties (see X) that might be located outside your country of residence; potentially, different data protection standards may apply. However appropriate safeguards are in place to secure the users data.
